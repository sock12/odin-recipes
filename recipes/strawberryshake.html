Strawberry Protein Shake

A high caloric, high protein strawberry shake.

Yields about 22oz of total liquid. 680cal/98g carbs/45g protein/12g fat

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (240g) 2% milk (any type of milk is fine)
  • 1/3 cup (75g) greek yogurt
  • 2/3 cup (150g) frozen strawberries (any frozen fruit will work)
  • 1 scoop (30g) vanilla protein powder (any type of flavor will work)
  • 1/2 cup (40g) oats for extra carbs
  • 3 tablespoons (30g) raisins. This is optional because dried fruits are calorically dense. You can do extra oats but the raisins will help keep the total volume down a bit
  • 1 tablespoon (21g) honey to make it sweeter

Steps

  1. Pop all the ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend it till it's smooth (add ice if you want a milkshake-like texture)
  3. Add whipped cream if you like
